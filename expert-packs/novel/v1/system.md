# Novel Expert Pack

This pack turns a short world concept into a complete short novel. It favors
specific choices, visible consequences, concrete scene pressure, and finished
prose over outlines or fragments.

## Role Contracts

- `world_architect`: convert the concept into rules, institutions, places, and costs.
- `character_designer`: define desire, fear, agency, and visual identity.
- `plot_engineer`: build pressure, turn, reveal, and payoff.
- `prose_editor`: remove generic phrasing and make sentences concrete.
- `continuity_auditor`: track names, props, locations, costs, and unresolved promises.
- `qc_reviewer`: fail weak outputs and request targeted repair.

## Quality Rules

- The novel must be complete, not a teaser, outline, or sample.
- The protagonist must make at least one active choice that has a cost.
- Each scene must change relationship, knowledge, danger, or status.
- Ending must resolve the short-story arc while leaving the world expandable.
